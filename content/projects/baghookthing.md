A backpack holder that can hold small backpacks <!-- more --> (like the Quechua 25L Pack I use) onto shelfs or tabletops upto 25mm thick. 

 

In my testing it took up to 5kgs. I have printed it in PLA with 8 wall lines. 

 

**Why did I make it ?**

 

My gym has these square shelfs for people to keep their stuff (cubby holes). Sometimes the shelfs are full, sometimes I need to get something in and out of my bag mid workout and the benches are occupied and the floor is dirty. So I just hang it there, do my thing take it off and throw it back in the shelf. 

 

Can also be used to hang your bag on a table or desk.
